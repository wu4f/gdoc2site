Honors Track

The Computer Science Honors Program provides a valuable opportunity for qualified students to research a specialty area within Computer Science with a faculty advisor.

Admission Requirements

  • Completion of CS Honors program form
  • Admission to the CS Upper Division Program
  • Minimum overall 3.5 PSU GPA and minimum 3.5 GPA in CS courses

Exceptions to these requirements may be approved by the department given sufficient documentation of extenuating circumstances; please contact an advisor for further information.

Program Requirements

Upon acceptance to the CS Honors program, the student must declare a research area of interest within Computer Science no later than the beginning of their Senior year (preferably by Spring quarter of Junior year). The student should identify a faculty member who is willing to serve as their thesis advisor, and this appointment will then be confirmed by the Department Chair. CS Honors theses will follow ACM document guidelines for style and formatting. The Honors thesis research is usually conducted after completing the course requirements of the student's Junior year.

Graduation Requirements

  • Completion of a written Honors thesis, in conjunction with a faculty advisor, with a minimum grade of B+.
  • Presentation of research to CS faculty and students in a seminar format.
  • Minimum overall 3.5 PSU GPA

Note: Honors Thesis (CS 403) may qualify as an approved CS elective.
