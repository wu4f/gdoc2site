Graduate AI Certificate

  • 2026-2027 Bulletin

The demand for artificial intelligence expertise is exploding across both industry and academia. The Graduate Certificate in Artificial Intelligence delivers specialized knowledge in both classical and modern Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning with applications in generative AI, natural language processing, and visual computing.

The certificate requires admission into the program and consists of 18 credits of graduate classes. There is one core class for a total of 3 hours. In addition, five elective classes must be taken for the needed additional 15 credit hours.

Required:

  • CS 545 Machine Learning

Select 5 from:

  • CS 517 Artificial Intelligence and Ethics
  • CS 527 Introduction to Visual Computing
  • CS 539 Introduction to Computational Imaging
  • CS 540 Deep Learning: Computational Structures and Programming
  • CS 541 Classical Artificial Intelligence
  • CS 543 Large Language Models
  • CS 544 Natural Language Processing
  • CS 546 Reinforcement Learning
  • CS 549 Data Clustering
  • CS 566 Voice Assistants
  • CS 575 Generative Security Application Engineering
  • Approved AI electives
